ICE · Fashion Low Back Brace (iF 2023)

ICE is a fashionable back brace for people with low back disorder problems. It can show immediate results and allow you to create long-term healthy habits. Improving your posture has positive effects on your well-being, self-esteem, and the impression you make on others. This product can control the tension of the brace anywhere at any time with two fingers. With controllable carbon wire, it can help you to adjust your posture properly, and keep your body in the correct movement. When you exercise, you can walk, run, jump, or do whatever you want with ICE. You can also check the tension with colors on the back. Take off your medical gear, ICE will be your fashion item for your low back.

Iceberg (2022)

Paying respect to the voice of this generation, fashion brands are inclined to focus on the sustainable performance of the materials and seek solutions to create environmentally friendly products. In this context, fashion industry for instance has been the face of profuse pro-environmental steps to play the role of a supporting role responsibly. With sustainable fashion becoming the headline of multifarious fashion designers and magazines, fashion industry also has sprung into action. In this project, we take a more bold step — we use the changes in the ozone holes’ shape as a generative design material for raising awareness and calling for action.

RunwayCLO (2021)

In this project, I hope to explore a wayto customisegraphic patterns on fashion clothes. In this case, I used style transferfor graphic design and chroma key for virtual try-on to show the designpatterns on the clothes. In this project, I have developed a real-time simulation that can be identifiedby a different colour on the clothes. The project includes three main steps: fashion trends forecasting, graphics and pattern style transfer, and chromakeying for clothing simulation.

Plus-size Block (2019)

The panel block supports a favourable preparation for plus-size block making. The application of the virtual operating system to generate a set of blocks has the advantages of saving time and cost. Furthermore, the system is capable of providing a more intelligent formula mode to enhance accuracy and efficiency.
